About

FlowMint

FlowMint builds tools for developers and QA teams. FlowMint Recorder lets you record browser tests and export them to the language and framework you use. FlowMint Test Evidence captures actions with screenshots and exports reports for documentation. All data stays on your device; we don’t collect or transmit your tests.

We’re working on more products to help you automate and ship with confidence.
